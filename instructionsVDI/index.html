Did you know you can use Microsoft Teams in VDI?

While the VDI desktop image has Skype for Business installed, that doesnt mean you cannot use Teams in a VDI desktop. How do you use Teams in VDI?

Before you can use Teams, youll need to install it. Once installed, Teams will be available every time you log into VDI. Visit https://teams.microsoft.com/downloads and download the desktop installer.

Run the file

You will see a Teams splash screen as the application installs.

Once Teams is installed, youll be prompted to log in. Enter your user ID in the format username@windstream.com and click Sign in

If prompted for your password, enter it.

In the upper right-hand corner of your screen, click on your avatar. Select Settings

To avoid adversely impacting VDI performance, un-check the Auto-start application box.

Click on Devices and verify that your local computers devices have been mapped through to the VDI session this allows you to participate in meetings and calls from the VDI desktop.

Close the settings window. Youre in Teams!

 

Now use it . If youre not sure what to do in Teams, check out our Stream posts, the Network Business Intelligence recorded training sessions, or Microsofts written and video training.
